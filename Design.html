Distinguished first title. It will blow your mind.

The human face is a very complex and distinct part of his body. In fact, it is one of the most complex signaling systems we have available; It includes more than 40 independent muscles, both structurally and functionally, so that each of them can be operated independently of the other; It is one of the strongest indicators of emotions.

Oh yeah, that's good. See for yourself.

When we laugh or cry, we display our emotions, allowing others to peek into our mind while "reading" our faces based on changes in key facial components such as the eyes, eyebrows, eyelids, nose, and lips.

Finally, this. Checkmate.

All muscles in our body are supported by nerves connected from all parts of the body to the spinal cord and brain. This neural communication is bidirectional, meaning that the nerve causes muscle contractions based on brain signals, and at the same time sends information about the state of the muscles to the brain
